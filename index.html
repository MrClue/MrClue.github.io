What is KittyCat Token?

KittyCat (KTC) is a community driven meme-coin with a focus on passive income. Starting with a supply of 1 trillion, we have burned 50% to a dead wallet to create an ever-lasting "black hole ". This way the token supply will always be decreasing, creating an ever-rising price floor.

Why should I hold KTC?

By holding KTC you will earn passive income in the form of BNB , every time someone buys/sells/sends KTC tokens. These rewards get automatically airdropped to your wallet in 60-minute cycles. You dont have to manually claim or connect your wallet to third party websites. Just sit back, relax & enjoy!

Furthermore, the KittyCat contract is designed in a way, that makes the token deflationary . This means that the token supply will reduce over time, increasing the value of the KTC tokens in your bags.

Technical Questions

How do I receive my rewards?

All BNB rewards gets airdropped automatically in 60-minute cycles. However, if the volume is low at a particular point in time, the rewards may take longer to be airdropped, (you still get the correct amount of rewards no matter what).

If you dont want to wait, you can also manually claim your token rewards directly on BSC-Scan.

Is there a minimum amount of $KTC tokens needed?

To counter botted attacks on our contract or any type of contract abuse, each holder must own at least 10,000 KTC tokens in order to receive rewards.
